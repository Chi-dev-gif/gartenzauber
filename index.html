About GreenScape Pro

Rooted in Passion, Grown Through Dedication

Founded in 2001 by landscape architect Marcus Cole, GreenScape Pro has grown from a two-person operation into a 60-strong team of horticulturists, designers, and craftspeople. We believe every outdoor space has untapped potential.

🏅

Award-Winning Design Team

Recipients of the National Landscape Industry Award three years running, recognized for innovative and sustainable design.

♻️

Eco-Conscious Practices

We use only organic fertilizers, native plantings, and water-wise techniques to protect local ecosystems.

🔐

Fully Licensed & Insured

Every team member is background-checked, certified, and covered under full liability insurance for your peace of mind.
